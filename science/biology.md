### Biochemical pathways and syntheses

Central dogma pathways:

- replication
- transcription
- translation

Cellular respiration pathways:

- glycolysis
- citric acid cycle
- oxidative phosphorylation

Photosynthesis pathways:

- Calvin cycle (dark reactions)
- Z-scheme(light reactions)
- C4 plants
- C3 plants
- CAM plants
- photorespiration

Biomolecule classes:

- Lipids
- Proteins
- Nucleic Acids
- Carbohydrates
